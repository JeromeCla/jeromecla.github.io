Challenges and Added Value

In the today packing industry, the requirements on surface quality and part accuracy is extremely high. Especially in the PET bottle manufacturing an excellent shape accuracy and a shiny surface finish on the mold section is essential. In the current manufacturing process of blow bottle molds, a polishing process is required to achieve these quality needs. Today we are able to substitute this polishing process using a state of the art high speed milling machine such as the XSM600ULP, adapted milling strategies and a high quality tools. This reduces the production costs of a mold and simplifies the production chain for our customers.
